# Journal Writing Skill: American Economic Review

## Journal Profile
- **ABS 2024 Rating:** 4*
- **Field:** ECON
- **Publisher:** American Economic Association
- **Scope:** General economics; publishes across all fields. The flagship journal of the AEA. Accepts empirical, theoretical, and structural papers.
- **Guidelines URL:** https://www.aeaweb.org/journals/aer/submissions/guidelines and https://www.aeaweb.org/journals/aer/style-guide
- **Field baseline:** econ-field-writing-style
- **Articles spot-checked:** 2 (Khanna et al., "Abundance from Abroad," AER 2026 [NBER WP 29862 pre-acceptance version]; Bordalo, Gennaioli, Shleifer, Terry, "Real Credit Cycles," AER 2026 [NBER WP 28416 pre-acceptance version])

## Hard Constraints (Author Guidelines)

> These override FIELD_SKILL where they conflict.

### Structure

No specific required section sequence beyond what FIELD_SKILL establishes. One structural constraint with no FIELD_SKILL analogue: **the introductory section must carry no heading**. All subsequent sections are numbered with Roman numerals. ENFORCED.

In-paper appendices begin on a new page after the main text and **before** the references section. They are typeset and counted toward the page total. Supplemental appendices are posted separately online and are not edited or typeset. The main paper must be self-contained; essential methods, data sources, and proofs belong in the paper, not the supplemental materials. ENFORCED.

### Length

Recommended maximum **40 pages** (11-point font, 1.5 spacing, 1-inch margins) or **45 pages** (12-point font). This count includes figures, tables, references, and in-paper appendices. Average published length is 35–36 typeset pages. Publication fee: $15 per typeset page. ENFORCED.

### Abstract

Unstructured. Must not exceed **100 words**. Required for all articles, comments, and replies. ENFORCED.

### Citations

Chicago Manual of Style Author-Date. In-text: list all authors for works with 1–4 authors; use first author + "et al." for 5 or more. Reference list: list all authors if 10 or fewer; if 11 or more, list the first seven + "et al." Data sources and registered studies must appear in the reference list. ENFORCED.

### Headings

Major sections: **Roman numerals** (I., II., III., …). First subsection level: **capital letters** (A., B., C., …). The introductory section receives no heading of any kind. ENFORCED.

This is a delta from FIELD_SKILL, which treats Roman and Arabic numerals as equally acceptable.

### Tables and Figures

Tables: maximum 9 columns including row headings; vertical orientation only; horizontal rules only (no vertical lines, no shading); no abbreviations in column headings; significance asterisks prohibited—report standard errors in parentheses instead; table footnotes use lowercase letter keys (a, b, c); zero before decimal point (0.357 not .357); panel labels as "Panel A," "Panel B," etc. ENFORCED.

Figures: vector formats only (PDF, EPS, AI, WMF, or PPT). Panel labels use letters in filename (Figure1a, Figure1b). ENFORCED.

### Explicit Prohibitions

- Significance asterisks in tables are **prohibited**. Standard errors must be reported in parentheses. ENFORCED.
- Papers simultaneously under review elsewhere are ineligible.
- Previously rejected AER papers cannot be resubmitted.
- AI software cannot be listed as author; AI use must be disclosed during submission.
- Third-party copyrighted images cannot be reprinted without written consent.

## Observed Deltas from FIELD_SKILL

> Only dimensions where this journal deviates from the field baseline.

### Statistical Reporting

**Field norm:** FIELD_SKILL specifies quantitative precision and economic significance alongside statistical significance but does not address significance stars.

**AER practice:** Significance asterisks are explicitly prohibited. All tables must report standard errors in parentheses as the sole indicator of precision. Both spot-checked working papers used significance stars (*** p<0.01, ** p<0.05, * p<0.10) in their pre-acceptance versions alongside parenthetical standard errors—confirming that stars must be removed at the accepted-article stage, not at submission.

**Classification:** ENFORCED (required for accepted articles; optional at submission).

### Structural Deviations

**Field norm:** FIELD_SKILL notes that section headings use Roman or Arabic numerals; either is acceptable. It does not address whether the Introduction carries a heading.

**AER practice:** (1) Roman numerals only for major sections. (2) The Introduction carries no heading whatsoever—it begins immediately after the abstract/JEL block. Both working paper versions used Arabic numerals (1, 2, 3 …), consistent with the style guide's statement that formatting is optional at submission but required for accepted articles.

**Classification:** ENFORCED.

**Field norm:** FIELD_SKILL describes the appendix as "a standalone appendix or online appendix" without distinguishing structural types.

**AER practice:** Two distinct appendix types with different rules. In-paper appendices (designated A, B, C) are typeset, placed before references, and counted in the page total. Supplemental appendices are posted unedited online and do not count toward the page limit. The main paper must be self-contained.

**Classification:** ENFORCED.

### Other Journal-Specific Patterns

**Citation author threshold:** FIELD_SKILL states author-year citation as the norm without specifying the threshold at which "et al." replaces full author lists. AER specifies: 1–4 authors listed in full; 5+ authors use "et al." in text; reference list lists all authors up to 10, uses first seven + "et al." for 11+. ENFORCED.

**Data and code availability:** A replication package (data and code) is required before acceptance for all empirical, simulation, or experimental work. FIELD_SKILL does not address replication requirements. ENFORCED.

**AI disclosure:** Use of AI tools in preparing the manuscript must be disclosed during submission; AI cannot be credited as an author. FIELD_SKILL does not address this. ENFORCED.

**Field experiment registration:** Papers involving field experiments must be registered in the AEA RCT registry; the identification number must be supplied at submission and cited in the acknowledgements footnote. ENFORCED (applies when relevant).

**Submission fee:** $200–$300 depending on AEA membership status and country income classification. Not a writing convention but a practical constraint.

## Common Pitfalls

1. **Significance stars in tables.** Both spot-checked pre-acceptance versions used stars; they must be stripped at the accepted-article stage. Submitting a revised manuscript with stars remaining after acceptance will delay typesetting.

2. **Heading on the Introduction.** Authors accustomed to Arabic numbering (common in working papers and other journals) will label the introduction "1. Introduction." AER requires it to be unlabeled entirely.

3. **Roman numerals.** Arabic numerals are near-universal in economics working papers. The switch to Roman numerals (I., II., A., B.) is required only for accepted articles but should be anticipated during revision.

4. **Page count including appendices.** The 40–45 page limit includes in-paper appendices and references. Authors who place extensive robustness material in an in-paper appendix may inadvertently breach the limit; moving material to a supplemental appendix (posted separately) is the remedy, but the main paper must remain self-contained.
