# Journal Writing Skill: Journal of Law and Economics

## Journal Profile
- **ABS 2024 Rating:** 3
- **Field:** ECON
- **Publisher:** University of Chicago Press
- **Scope:** Economic analysis of law, regulation, industrial organization, antitrust policy, political economy of legislation, and law and finance. Publishes both empirical and theoretical work; does not publish book reviews.
- **Guidelines URL:** https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/journals/jle/instruct

## Hard Constraints (Author Guidelines)

> These override FIELD_SKILL where they conflict.

### Structure
Follows FIELD_SKILL norm. No section ordering mandated beyond standard economics practice.

### Length
No explicit manuscript word or page limit stated in accessible guidelines.

### Abstract
Unstructured; **strict 150-word limit** enforced at submission. The title page must include names, e-mail addresses, and affiliations of all authors plus the abstract.
Label: **ENFORCED**

### Citations
Author-date (author-year) in text; full references in a reference list. Aligns with FIELD_SKILL. For submission, any reasonable citation style is accepted; the journal's typeset style sheets apply only after acceptance. The journal follows **The Chicago Manual of Style, 16th edition** for general matters of style.
Label: **ENFORCED** (for accepted manuscripts); no delta from FIELD_SKILL citation style norm.

### Headings
Follows FIELD_SKILL norm.

### Tables and Figures
In the submitted manuscript file, tables must appear **after** the reference list (order: text → footnotes → appendixes → reference list → tables → figure legends → figures). Tables are not embedded in text at submission. Tables follow Chicago Manual of Style chapter 13. All elements (including footnotes, tables, and figure legends) must be **double-spaced**.
Label: **ENFORCED**

### Explicit Prohibitions
- Cross-references to footnotes elsewhere in the article are **prohibited**.
- Book reviews are not published.
- Papers under consideration elsewhere or previously published are not accepted.
- **Submission fee required**: $100 for non-subscribers; $75 for individual subscribers. This is unusual among economics journals and applies at initial submission.

## Observed Deltas from FIELD_SKILL

> Only dimensions where this journal deviates from the field baseline.

### Abstract Length
**Field norm:** FIELD_SKILL does not specify word limits for any journal.
**This journal:** 150-word strict limit, enforced at submission stage.
**Classification:** ENFORCED

### Tables Placement (Submission Format)
**Field norm:** FIELD_SKILL is silent on submission file organisation; tables are typically discussed as embedded in published text.
**This journal:** Submitted manuscripts must place tables after the reference list, not embedded in the text body. This is a submission-format requirement, not a typeset norm, but it affects how working versions of manuscripts should be structured when preparing for submission.
**Classification:** ENFORCED

### Data Replication Policy
**Field norm:** FIELD_SKILL explicitly excludes replication and data availability requirements as journal-specific.
**This journal:** Empirical papers, simulations, and experimental work must provide data, programs, and computational details sufficient for replication **prior to publication** — not merely as a stated commitment. Non-compliance blocks publication.
**Classification:** ENFORCED

### Submission Fee
**Field norm:** Not covered by FIELD_SKILL; no standard norm exists in the field.
**This journal:** $100 (non-subscriber) / $75 (subscriber) fee at submission. This is a non-trivial financial threshold that distinguishes this outlet from most economics journals.
**Classification:** ENFORCED (journal-specific practice with no field analogue)

### Footnote Cross-Reference Prohibition
**Field norm:** FIELD_SKILL does not address internal footnote cross-references.
**This journal:** Explicitly prohibited from cross-referencing footnotes elsewhere in the article. Authors who rely on "see footnote N" constructions elsewhere in the manuscript must restructure.
**Classification:** ENFORCED

## Common Pitfalls

1. **Abstract over 150 words.** The limit is strict and applied at submission. Cut the abstract before uploading — it will not be trimmed for you.
2. **Tables embedded in text.** The submission file must order elements as: text → footnotes → appendixes → reference list → tables → figure legends → figures. Embedding tables in the body violates the submission format requirement.
3. **No replication package ready.** Accepted empirical papers must supply data and code *before* publication, not after. Prepare the replication archive during revision, not after acceptance.
4. **Footnote cross-references.** Constructions such as "see note 12 above" or "cf. footnote 7" are explicitly prohibited. Any substantive point in a footnote must stand alone or be consolidated into the text.
