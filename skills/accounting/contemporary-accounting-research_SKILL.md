# Journal Writing Skill: Contemporary Accounting Research

## Journal Profile
- **ABS 2024 Rating:** 4
- **Field:** ACCOUNT
- **Publisher:** Wiley / Canadian Academic Accounting Association (CAAA)
- **Scope:** Broad accounting research (archival, experimental, qualitative, interpretive) covering accounting's role within organizations, markets, and society. Published quarterly.
- **Guidelines URL:** https://www.caaa.ca/car-info-for-authors
- **Field baseline:** account-field-writing-style
- **Articles spot-checked:** 1 (Pappas, Walker, Xu & Zeng, "Government subsidies and income smoothing," CAR 41(3), 2024, DOI: 10.1111/1911-3846.12947)

## Hard Constraints (Author Guidelines)

> These override FIELD_SKILL where they conflict.

### Structure
Follows FIELD_SKILL norm.

### Length
**ENFORCED.** Maximum 30 pages of main text (12-point font, double-spaced, 8.5" × 11", 1-inch margins). Overall submission cap of 50 pages (excluding title page, abstract, and references). Qualitative papers may occasionally exceed the 30-page main text limit provided the 50-page overall cap is maintained.

### Abstract
**ENFORCED.** Maximum 300 words. Unstructured single paragraph. Must include: research question and its importance, predictions (if applicable), methods employed, principal findings, and key implications. Followed by up to six keywords and JEL classification codes.

**Bilingual abstract required: ENFORCED.** All published articles must include both an English abstract and a French-language translation of the abstract. This is a standing CAAA mandate observed consistently in published work. Authors should prepare both versions at submission.

### Citations
**ENFORCED.** APA author-date format throughout, which differs from the Chicago author-date variant used at JAE/JAR. Specific markers:
- In-text: comma between author and year — `(Cahan, 1992)` not `(Cahan 1992)`.
- Multiple citations in one parenthetical use semicolons in alphabetical order: `(Cahan, 1992; Grace & Leverty, 2010; Han & Wang, 1998)`.
- Ampersand ("&") joins co-authors in parenthetical citations: `(Grace & Leverty, 2010)`.
- Reference list: APA 7th edition format — `Author, A. B., & Author, C. D. (Year). Title. Journal Name, Volume(Issue), pages.` — with year in parentheses directly after authors.

### Headings
Published articles use Arabic section numbers with ALL-CAPS heading text and a pipe separator (e.g., `1 | INTRODUCTION`, `2 | BACKGROUND`, `3 | HYPOTHESIS DEVELOPMENT`; subsections as `4.1 | Sample and data`). This is the Wiley published-output format for this journal; manuscript authors should confirm the required submission format in the CAR Style Guide. **PRACTICE ONLY** for the specific pipe/ALL-CAPS combination; Arabic numerals are confirmed over Roman.

### Tables and Figures
Follows FIELD_SKILL norm. Tables report t-statistics in parentheses below coefficients (confirmed in spot-check). Significance stars at 10% (*), 5% (**), 1% (***), two-tailed.

### Explicit Prohibitions
- Simultaneous submission to another journal is prohibited.
- **ENFORCED.** Any use of generative AI in drafting, research design, or analysis must be declared during submission (mandatory as of January 1, 2024). AI use in research design or methods must be described in the Methods section. Spelling/grammar tools and reference managers are exempt.
- **ENFORCED.** Ethics approval (IRB or institutional equivalent) must be verified and uploaded for all research involving human participants (experiments, interviews, surveys).

## Observed Deltas from FIELD_SKILL

### Bilingual Abstract
**FIELD_SKILL norm:** Single-language abstract (English assumed).
**CAR:** Both an English abstract and a French translation are published as part of every article. The French abstract mirrors the English in structure and content. This is the most significant journal-specific delta. Authors should arrange translation before submission. **ENFORCED.**

### Citation Format
**FIELD_SKILL norm:** Author-date in-text; does not specify APA vs. Chicago for in-text punctuation or reference-list format.
**CAR:** Explicitly APA 7th edition. In-text citations include a comma between author name and year `(Author, Year)`. Reference lists use APA format with year in parentheses after authors and "&" joining co-authors. JAE/JAR use Chicago author-date (no comma; "and" not "&"). Switching format from another ACCOUNT journal produces a non-trivial delta. **ENFORCED.**

### Length Constraints
**FIELD_SKILL norm:** Does not specify; length constraints are called out as journal-specific.
**CAR:** Hard ceiling of 30 pages main text / 50 pages total. Qualitative papers get limited flexibility on the main text limit. **ENFORCED.**

## Common Pitfalls

1. **Omitting the French abstract.** Submitting only an English abstract is the most likely CAR-specific oversight. Prepare the French translation before submission, not after acceptance.

2. **Using Chicago author-date citation format.** Authors switching from JAE, JAR, or TAR may write `(Cahan 1992)` without a comma, or use "and" instead of "&" in co-author citations. CAR requires APA-style punctuation throughout.

3. **Exceeding the 30-page main text limit.** The 30-page cap on body text is strict. If your paper has extensive robustness tables, place them in appendices or online supplementary material — the 50-page overall cap still applies to the full submission file.

4. **Failing to declare generative AI use.** The AI declaration requirement (effective January 2024) is mandatory for new submissions. Omitting it at the point of submission will delay processing.
